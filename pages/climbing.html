Climbing

This page exists because why not :D

I started climbing in Dec. 2021 when I was a junior in college. A friend of mine took me to the Central Rock Gym in Worcester and it has been nothing but joy ever since (well, I suppose there were some minor injuries).

I enjoy all kinds of climbing: boulder, sports, top rope, multi, and a growing interest in trad. I like exploring new crags and meeting fellow climbers along the way. :)
